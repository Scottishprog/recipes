Ingredients:
3 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1/8 lb cheese


Instructions:
1. Grate cheese
2. Warm up griddle
3. Break eggs into bowl, and thuroughly wisk together.
4. Add milk, and wisk in.
5. Pour mixture onto heated griddle, and allow to just solidify.
6. Flip the scrabled eggs over - make sure all parts are cooked.
7. Serve, clean griddle when it's hot.
